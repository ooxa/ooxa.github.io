Why Containerization?

Containerization allows your applications to run consistently across multiple computing environments, significantly enhancing deployment and scalability. It encapsulates an application along with its environment, dependencies, and configurations into a single, self-contained unit, known as a container. This ensures your application runs the same, no matter where it is deployed.
